Ezio Auditore da Firenze

Ezio wearing the Armor of Altair.

Ezio was a fictional character in Ubisoft's Assassin's Creed series. He was one of the most popular characters and was given the most screen time. Unlike other assassins, who only received one game each, Ezio was featured in his own trilogy.

Ezio was featured in the following games:

  1. Assassin's Creed II
  2. Assassin's Creed Brotherhood
  3. Assassin's Creed Revelations

Some of Ezio's accomplishments include

  • Surviving the Pazzi attack on his family.
  • Eliminating the Pazzi from Florence.
  • Removing the Borgia from power in Italy.
  • Discovering the Library of Altair Ibn La'Ahd
